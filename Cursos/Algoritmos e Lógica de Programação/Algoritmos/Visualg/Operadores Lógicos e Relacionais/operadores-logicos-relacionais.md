# Visualg - Operadores Lógicos e Relacionais

## Operadores Relacionais

- Maior que >
- Menor que <
- Maior ou igual a >=
- Menor ou igual a <=
- Igual a = 
- Diferende de <>

## Operadores Lógicos

Operador E (&):

```
_____________________________________
|      p     |     q     |   p E q  |
|____________|___________|__________|
|      V     |     V     |     V    |
|____________|___________|__________|
|      V     |     F     |     F    |
|____________|___________|__________|
|      F     |     V     |     F    |
|____________|___________|__________|
|      F     |     F     |     F    |
|____________|___________|__________|   

```

Operador OR (||):

```
_____________________________________
|      p     |     q     |   p OU q |
|____________|___________|__________|
|      V     |     V     |     V    |
|____________|___________|__________|
|      V     |     F     |     V    |
|____________|___________|__________|
|      F     |     V     |     V    |
|____________|___________|__________|
|      F     |     F     |     F    |
|____________|___________|__________|   

```

Operador NOT (!)

```
______________________
|     p    |  NOT p  |
|__________|_________|
|     V    |     F   |
|__________|_________|
|     F    |     V   |
|__________|_________|

```

|          Lógicos        | || OR  |
